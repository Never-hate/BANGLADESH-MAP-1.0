---

## 🔴 Interactive Map Details 🟢

Our map is designed to provide a seamless and immersive experience, reflecting the beauty and geographical diversity of Bangladesh (🇧🇩):

*   **Authentic Borders:** Real GeoJSON coordinates render the exact, winding boundaries of Bangladesh and its internal districts.
*   **Smart Recognition & Interactivity:** Click anywhere on the map, and the system instantly identifies the district, zooming in smoothly and updating the UI dynamically.
*   **Deep Dive Analytics:** Instantly view exact Geo-coordinates (Latitude & Longitude), total land area, current population, and a complete badge-styled list of all upazilas for the selected district.
*   **Immersive Glassmorphism:** A frosted-glass interface that blurs the background, bringing the map's vibrant colors forward in both Dark 🌙 and Light ☀️ modes.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Setup

- **Frontend:** Pure HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS
- **Mapping Library:** Leaflet.js
- **Data Source:** Custom GeoJSON shapefiles & JSON metadata.

To run locally, simply open `index.html` in your browser or use a Live Server extension. No complex build tools or hidden configurations are required!
